About me

A career shaped by
curiosity and craft.

I'm Carlos Ebrahim Vendrell — Barcelona-born, Denmark-based. I've spent the last 7+ years in Copenhagen and Aarhus exploring the intersection of AI, emerging technology, and the kind of restless curiosity that leads you to dive in the Red Sea one week and build an enterprise XR demo the next.

Based in Denmark, I build AI and XR projects at Grundfos while keeping the rest of life open to unexpected learning and travel. This site is a compact map of what I care about most — work, storytelling, and the places that keep me curious.
