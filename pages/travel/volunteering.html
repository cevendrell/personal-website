Volunteering

Since I was a teenager, I’ve used volunteering opportunities as a way to help out in different ways that I’m used to, meet people from very diverse backgrounds, and lately, as a way to travel and experience countries beyond conventional tourism.

For the last two years, I’ve been volunteering with All Hands and Hearts, a disaster relief organization that works alongside local residents to help families and communities recover by building safe, resilient schools, homes, and other community infrastructure.
